Characters in Noli Me Tangere
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin Crisostomo Ibarra, full name Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin, was a young student from San Diego. Following his father's death, he returned to his hometown and sought to establish a proper school there, only to face numerous obstacles from the local figures. Implicated in a revolt, he later fled the town and forged a new identity as the jeweler Simoun.
